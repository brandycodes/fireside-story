It was a dark and stormy night...

and there was a knock at the door...

She sighed and looked up from her laptop. Who could be here at this hour?

Setting the laptop aside, she activated the trap door under the front mat, nodding as she heard a clunk in the basement.

She listened for noises of surprise from below, from either the knocker or her pet, Fluffy.

She looked up as fluffy scampered over, towering over her, jumping up and down with excitement.
